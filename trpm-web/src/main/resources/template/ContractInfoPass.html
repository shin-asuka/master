But just before you rush off in excitement…

We believe that you are a responsible and professional teacher. However, we would still like to outline some basic expectations to ensure you can make the most of the VIPKID teaching platform.

 1. Always have good background (especially lighting);

 2. Make sure you have a good, stable internet connection, camera and headset;

 3. Always maintain high energy levels while teaching;

 4. Be very careful with your schedule, and always show up for your classes on time. Please be sure to return to your Teacher's Portal daily.

  Any time slots that you open can be booked by our students or head office staff. We won't book you with less than 24 hours’

  notice unless you give us permission to do so by checking the short notice booking boxes.

  If you do check these boxes, be sure to revisit your Teacher's Portal before your first available time slot to check if it has been booked!
